Personal Description

Describe yourself or someone you know

Lesson Review Game

What is a personal description?

Purpose
Describe a person — appearance, personality, what they do
Length
80–100 words
Structure
4 points: appearance, personality, job/hobby, opinion
Tenses
Mainly present simple

Language for descriptions

Appearance
She has... (dark hair, blue eyes) / He is... (tall, slim, athletic)
Personality
She is very... / He tends to be... / She can be quite...
Job / hobby
She works as a... / In his free time, he...
Opinion
I admire her/him because... / What I like most about her/him is...
Avoid repeating the same adjectives. Use a variety: kind, generous, determined, creative.

Model description

Example
This is my friend Carlos. He is tall with short dark hair. He is very funny and kind. He works as a chef. In his free time, he plays football. I admire him because he works very hard and always makes people laugh.
