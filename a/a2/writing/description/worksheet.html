Task

Write a description (80–100 words) of a person. Include: physical appearance, personality, what they do, and why you admire/like them.

Useful language
AppearanceShe/He has... / She/He is... (tall, dark-haired, etc.)
PersonalityShe/He is... (kind, funny, hardworking)
Job/hobbyShe/He works as... / In her/his free time she/he...
OpinionI admire her/him because...
