A2 · Writing

Postcard

Writing Task
Name: ___________________________    Date: _______________

Task

Write a postcard from a holiday (40–60 words). Say where you are, what the weather is like, and what you have done. End with a friendly closing.

Write approximately 60 words.

Model Answer
