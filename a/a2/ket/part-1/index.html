Matching

6 Qs · match notices to meanings

Part 1 — Matching

Six short real-world texts (notices, signs, labels, emails). Match each text to the correct meaning from 8 options.

Strategy

  • Read the text carefully — focus on the main message, not every word
  • Watch for numbers, times, and place words
  • Two options are distractors — they won't match anything
  • Time: about 8 minutes
