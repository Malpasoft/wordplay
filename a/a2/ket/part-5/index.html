Open Cloze

6 Qs · fill ONE word

Part 5 — Open Cloze

A short text with six gaps. Write ONE word in each gap (no options given). Tests small grammar words.

Strategy

  • Most answers are grammar words: a, the, in, on, at, is, was, to, for, with, and, but, so
  • Read the sentence around the gap aloud — what sounds right?
  • ONE word only — never write two words
  • Time: about 8 minutes
