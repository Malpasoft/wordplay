KET · Part 7

Part 7 Practice — Open Writing

Write a short message covering all three content points

Strategy & Tips Practice
YOUR TASK

You want to invite your English friend Sam to your birthday party. Write a note to Sam. Write 25–35 words.

Tell Sam:

  • when the party is
  • where the party is
  • what to bring
Words: 0 / 25–35
MODEL ANSWER (30 words)
Hi Sam,

I'm having a birthday party on Saturday at 7 pm. It's at my house — 15 Park Road. Please bring something to drink. I hope you can come!

[Your name]
CHECK YOUR WORK
  • I included when the party is (day and time).
  • I included where the party is (address or place).
  • I told Sam what to bring.
  • My note is between 25 and 35 words.
  • I used a friendly greeting and sign-off.
