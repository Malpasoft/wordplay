Open Writing

1 Q · write 25-35 words

Part 7 — Open Writing

Write a short message (25-35 words). Usually a note, email, or postcard. You must include three content points.

Strategy

  • Cover ALL three content points — you lose marks for missing any
  • Keep it simple — short sentences are fine at A2
  • Count your words: under 25 loses marks, over 35 wastes time
  • Time: about 15 minutes
