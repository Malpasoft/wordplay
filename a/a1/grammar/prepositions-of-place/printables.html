Location Phrases

Lesson Handout

in, on, at

in — inside a space — The cat is in the box.

on — on a surface — The book is on the table.

at — a specific point — She is at the door.

More prepositions

under — The dog is under the chair.

next to — The bank is next to the supermarket.

between — The café is between the park and the school.

behind — My bag is behind the door.

in front of — The car is in front of the house.

in / on / at with places

in — a city / country — She lives in London.

on — a road / street — The shop is on Green Street.

at — a building / place — He is at school.

Recap

in — inside · city · country

on — surface · street

at — point · building

Other — under · next to · between · behind
