Questions & short answers

QuestionYesNo
Am I...?Yes, you are.No, you aren't.
Is she / he / it...?Yes, she is.No, she isn't.
Are you / we / they...?Yes, I am. / Yes, they are.No, I'm not. / No, they aren't.

Short answers use the full form: "Yes, I am." — NOT "Yes, I'm." ✗
