Possessive Adjectives

Lesson Handout

What are possessive adjectives?

Use — show that something belongs to someone

I — my — That is my book.

You — your — Is this your pen?

He — his — That is his bag.

All possessive adjectives

I — my

You — your

He — his

She — her

It — its

We — our

They — their

Important: his vs her vs its

his — belongs to a male — That’s his car.

her — belongs to a female — That’s her bag.

its — belongs to a thing/animal — The dog wagged its tail.

Warning — its ≠ it’s (it’s = it is)

Recap

Singular — my · your · his · her · its

Plural — our · your · their

Rule — Use before a noun. They never change form.
