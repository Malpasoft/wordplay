Can and Can’t

Lesson Handout

What does 'can' mean?

Ability — She can swim very fast.

Inability — I can’t play the piano.

Permission — Can I use your phone?

Form

Rule — can + base verb (same for all subjects)

Positive — I / She / They can swim.

Negative — I / She / They can’t swim.

Question — Can you / she / they swim?

Warning — Never: ‘can to swim’ or ‘cans’

Short answers

Yes — Yes, I can.

No — No, I can’t.

Example — Can you cook? — Yes, I can.

Recap

Positive — can + base verb

Negative — can’t / cannot + base verb

Question — Can + subject + base verb?

Rule — No -s ever — She can (NOT cans)
