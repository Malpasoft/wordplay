1. Who is the data controller?

Em, operating Word Play. Contact: emi.dom123@gmail.com

3. Local storage

Progress data is also stored in your browser's localStorage. This is first-party functional storage necessary for the service to work offline. No consent banner is required for strictly necessary functional storage under the ePrivacy Directive.

4. Who sees your data

  • Your teacher (Em) can see your email, level, lesson bookings, and progress scores for the purpose of teaching you.
  • Cloudflare, Inc. stores and processes data as our infrastructure provider (Pages hosting, D1 database). Data is stored in the EU (Dublin region where available). Cloudflare is our data processor under a DPA.
  • We do not sell data or share it with any other third parties.

5. Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time (see Section 7). Rate-limiting logs are purged automatically after 24 hours. After account deletion, backups are removed within 30 days.

6. Transfers outside the EU

Cloudflare may process some data outside the EEA. They rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for such transfers. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access — download all your data: sign in and go to Dashboard → Account settings → Download my data.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all data from the same settings page.
  • Correction — contact us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Portability — your data export is in machine-readable JSON.
  • Object / Restrict — contact us to restrict processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Complaint — you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish data protection authority (AEPD): aepd.es.

To exercise any right, email emi.dom123@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Children

Word Play is not directed at children under 16. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be notified by email at least 14 days in advance. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates the current version.
