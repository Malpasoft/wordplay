Exercise 1
Choose the correct relative pronoun
8 points
1
The student who / which got the scholarship is studying environmental science.
2
The app who / that went viral was downloaded ten million times in a week.
3
The wildlife reserve where / which rare species live is protected by law.
4
The athlete whose / who personal best was broken won gold.
5
The device that / who I bought last year has already broken down.
6
The tutor that / where explained the curriculum was very clear.
7
The school where / that I attended had an excellent sports programme.
8
The journalist whose / which article went viral received thousands of comments.
Exercise 2
Combine the sentences

3

4

5

6
