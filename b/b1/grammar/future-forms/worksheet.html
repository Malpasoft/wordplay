Exercise 1
Which future form?
8 points
Choose the best future form.
1
Look at those pollution levels — the government will have to / is going to have to take action.
2
I think renewable energy will / is going to become the main power source by 2050.
3
"The Wi-Fi is down." "Don't worry, I 'll restart / 'm going to restart the router."
4
We are meeting / will meet the tutor at 10am — she confirmed the appointment.
5
I have decided — I 'm going to take up / 'll take up cycling to reduce my carbon footprint.
6
Scientists predict that biodiversity will continue / is continuing to decline unless action is taken.
7
She is checking in / will check in on Friday — she booked it weeks ago.
8
"I can't carry these bags." "Don't worry, I 'll help / 'm going to help you."
