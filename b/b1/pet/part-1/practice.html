Match each person to the text that suits them best

Strategy & Tips Practice
Part 1 — Match each person (1–5) to the text (A–H) that would be most useful for them. Three texts are not used.

Read what each person wants, then choose the best text for them.

PERSON 1 — MARIA
Maria loves cooking and wants to join a class where she can learn new recipes from different countries around the world.
PERSON 2 — TOM
Tom wants to get fit and is looking for something active he can do in the morning before he starts work.
PERSON 3 — LUCIA
Lucia is learning a new language and wants an activity that will specifically help her improve her reading in that language.
PERSON 4 — JAMES
James has just moved to a new city and is looking to meet people who enjoy playing strategy and board games.
PERSON 5 — AISHA
Aisha is a college student and needs to find a part-time job with flexible hours that fits around her studies.
TEXTS
AInternational Cooking School: Join our world cuisine workshops on Tuesday evenings. Learn to cook authentic dishes from Asia, Africa, and South America. All skill levels welcome. Equipment provided.
BLanguage Exchange Library: We meet every Saturday morning at the Central Library. Bring a book in your target language and practise reading it with a native-speaker partner.
CSunrise Fitness Club: Morning Bootcamp sessions run Monday–Friday at 6:30 am. High-energy workouts to start your day. No prior experience needed — all fitness levels welcome.
DBoard Game Café: New members always welcome! We play strategy, cooperative, and classic board games together. We meet on the first Friday of each month. Free to join — no registration needed.
EWeekend Waitress/Waiter Wanted: Busy city-centre restaurant seeks friendly weekend staff. Flexible hours — choose the shifts that suit you. Ideal for students with weekday commitments.
FCreative Writing Circle: Share your stories and get constructive feedback from fellow writers. We meet every Thursday evening at the Town Hall. All genres welcome.
GPhotography Club: Join us for monthly photo walks around the city. Beginners and experienced photographers both welcome. Share your images on our group gallery online.
HEvening Language Classes: Improve your grammar and conversation skills in French, Spanish, or German. Beginner to advanced levels. Classes run on Monday and Wednesday evenings. Register online.
