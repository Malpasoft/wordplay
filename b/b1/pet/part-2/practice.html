Part 2 — Four people describe their visit to a new science museum. For questions 1–5, choose the correct person (A–D). You may choose any letter more than once.
REVIEWS
A — Tom The exhibition was spectacular, but I left feeling slightly overwhelmed. There was simply too much to take in during a single visit. I definitely plan to return and will prepare a plan of what to see beforehand.
B — Sara I brought my children hoping they would enjoy the interactive exhibits, and they absolutely loved it. They spent over an hour at the volcano model alone. It was a brilliant day out for the whole family.
C — Nadia I was disappointed by the lack of support for younger visitors. The text panels assumed a high level of scientific knowledge, which made it very difficult for my children to follow along and stay engaged.
D — Alex I went mainly for the space section, which exceeded my expectations. The new planetarium show was absolutely stunning. I was less impressed by the history of science rooms, which felt dated and in need of updating.

Which person …

1
says one part of the museum needs to be modernised?
2
plans to visit the museum again?
3
says children particularly enjoyed the hands-on activities?
4
felt that the written information was too complex for general visitors?
5
was particularly impressed by one specific attraction?
