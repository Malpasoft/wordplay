Adjective + Preposition

Worksheet
Name: ___________________________    Date: _______________

Add the correct preposition (10 points)

1. She is very good ___________ organising her revision schedule.
2. He is worried ___________ missing the deadline for his assignment.
3. Are you interested ___________ studying for an environmental science degree?
4. She is tired ___________ seeing so much litter on the streets every day.
5. The whole team is proud ___________ winning the regional championship.
6. She is afraid ___________ public speaking but she is taking a course to overcome it.
7. He is responsible ___________ managing the social media accounts of the charity.
8. I am not very keen ___________ early morning training sessions.
9. We were surprised ___________ the impact that recycling has had on local carbon emissions.
10. Students should be aware ___________ the plagiarism policy before submitting coursework.

Complete the sentences (10 points)

Fill in both the adjective and the preposition.

11. She is ______ ___________ wildlife photography — she goes out every weekend with her camera. (keen)
12. He is quite ______ ___________ maths but excellent at languages. (bad)
13. They are ______ ___________ the damage that deforestation is causing. (aware)
14. She was ______ ___________ how high her screen time was this week. (surprised)
15. He is ______ ___________ the same routine every day — he needs a new challenge. (tired)
