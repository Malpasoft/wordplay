Sport and Leisure

Championship, referee, personal best, team spirit.

Competitions · Performance · Leisure activities
B1 Vocabulary

Sport Vocabulary

championship
a competition to decide who is the best
referee
the official who enforces the rules in a sport
spectator
someone who watches a sporting event
opponent
the person or team you are competing against
personal best
your fastest or best performance in a sport
team spirit
the feeling of loyalty and cooperation within a team

Competition vocabulary

compete
take part in a competition
score
achieve a goal or point in a sport
beat
defeat an opponent
draw
finish a match with equal scores
trophy
an award given to the winner
training session
a period of practice to improve performance

Leisure vocabulary

take up
start a new hobby or sport
hobby
an activity you do for pleasure in your free time
gym
a place with equipment for physical exercise
keep fit
stay in good physical condition
workout
a period of physical exercise

Collocations

win / lose a match / a championship
set a personal best / a record
take up a sport / a hobby
do a workout / exercise
go to the gym / training
