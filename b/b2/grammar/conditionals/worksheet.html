Exercise 3
Key word transformation — FCE Part 4
10 points
1
We didn't invest in renewable energy, so we still rely on fossil fuels. INVESTED
If we ______ in renewable energy, we wouldn't still rely on fossil fuels.
2
She won't get the promotion unless she improves her work ethic. UNLESS
______ her work ethic, she won't get the promotion.
3
I regret that the company didn't act on the data privacy breach sooner. ACTED
If the company ______ on the breach sooner, the damage would have been limited.
4
Companies that don't reduce emissions will face heavy penalties. REDUCE
If companies ______ their emissions, they will face heavy penalties.
5
More workers would choose freelance careers if there were more stability. WERE
If ______ more stability, more workers would choose freelance careers.
