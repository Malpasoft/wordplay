Passive Voice (B2)

Reporting verbs · modal passive · passive infinitive

Passive: B2 depth

At B2, you extend the passive to reporting verbs, modal passives, and passive infinitive/gerund.

Review
be + PP: The car was stolen. Results will be announced.
Reporting verbs
It is said that... / He is believed to be...
Modal passive
This must be checked. / It should be done.

Passive with reporting verbs

It + is/was + reported/said/believed/thought/known + that + clause ✓
Type 1: It + passive
It is reported that the economy is improving.
Type 2: subject + passive + to
The economy is reported to be improving.
Common verbs
said · reported · believed · thought · known · considered · found · claimed · alleged

Modal passives

modal + be + past participle ✓
obligation
This form must be completed.
recommendation
The report should be revised.
possibility
The meeting might be cancelled.
ability
This problem can be solved.
past modal passive
It should have been done yesterday.

Passive infinitive and gerund

Passive infinitive
She wants to be promoted.
Passive gerund
Nobody likes being criticised.
Perfect passive infinitive
She was lucky to have been chosen.

Common mistakes

This should been done.
→
This should have been done.
modal + have + been + PP
Nobody likes to be criticise.
→
Nobody likes being criticised.
after like → -ing → passive gerund
