Worksheet
Name: ___________________________    Date: _______________

Choose the correct adjective (10 points)

1. There is ______ evidence that a sedentary lifestyle contributes to chronic illness.
☐ strong☐ powerful☐ heavy
2. There was a ______ increase in carbon emissions last decade.
☐ sharp☐ quick☐ fast
3. Politicians expressed ______ concern about the rise in inequality.
☐ deep☐ big☐ large
4. There is ______ unemployment in regions where automation has replaced factory work.
☐ high☐ big☐ large
5. Renewable energy plays a ______ role in addressing the climate crisis.
☐ crucial☐ important☐ big
6. The campaign aims to reach a ______ range of people across different social groups.
☐ broad☐ large☐ big
7. Automation is causing ______ disruption across many industries worldwide.
☐ widespread☐ general☐ common
8. There is a ______ relationship between poverty and poor mental health outcomes.
☐ close☐ near☐ tight
9. Scientists made a ______ breakthrough in renewable energy storage technology.
☐ major☐ big☐ important
10. There is ______ awareness of the link between social media use and mental health.
☐ growing☐ increasing☐ expanding
