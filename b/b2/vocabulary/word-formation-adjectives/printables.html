Word Formation — Adjectives

Worksheet
Name: ___________________________    Date: _______________

Form the correct adjective (10 points)

1. This packaging is made from fully ___________ materials. (SUSTAIN)
2. The new app is incredibly ___________ — even young children can use it. (ACCESS)
3. The rising cost of housing has made it ___________ for many young people. (AFFORD)
4. We need ___________ solutions to address the climate crisis. (INNOVATE)
5. The factory's methods were ___________ — they ignored all the safety regulations. (RESPONSIBLE)
6. The new policy has been completely ___________ — emissions have not fallen at all. (EFFECT)
7. The company was fined for ___________ data practices. (LEGAL)
8. She gave a very ___________ presentation on social mobility. (PROFESSION)
9. The situation is far too ___________ to solve overnight. (COMPLEX)
10. The findings were ___________ — nobody expected such results. (RELY)
