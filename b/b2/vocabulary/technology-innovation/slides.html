B2 Vocabulary

Technology Vocabulary

artificial intelligence (AI)
machines performing tasks that require human intelligence
automation
using technology to perform tasks without human involvement
cybersecurity
protecting systems and data from digital attacks
data privacy
the right to control how personal information is used
digital footprint
the data trail left by someone online

More technology vocabulary

innovation
the introduction of new ideas or methods
disruptive technology
technology that fundamentally changes an industry
algorithm
a set of rules a computer uses to solve problems
remote work
working from outside the office, usually from home
connectivity
the state of being connected, especially to the internet
redundancy
job loss caused by automation or restructuring

Key collocations

adopt / embrace new technology
protect data privacy
invest in cybersecurity
face redundancy / automation
