B2 Vocabulary

Society and Culture Vocabulary

inequality
unfair differences between groups in society
globalisation
the process of countries becoming more connected
integration
combining different groups into one society
discrimination
unfair treatment based on race, gender, age etc.
diversity
variety of different people or things in a group
minority
a smaller group within a larger population

More society vocabulary

welfare
the health, happiness and financial support of people
prejudice
an unfair opinion formed without enough knowledge
social mobility
the ability to move up or down in social class
community
a group of people living in the same area or sharing interests
cohesion
the quality of being united as a group
stigma
a mark of disgrace associated with a particular circumstance

Key collocations

tackle / address inequality
promote diversity and inclusion
reduce discrimination / prejudice
social mobility / cohesion / welfare
ethnic / cultural minority
