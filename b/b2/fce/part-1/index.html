Part 1 — Multiple-choice Cloze

8 questions · vocabulary in context · 4 options each

What does Part 1 test?

Part 1 tests your vocabulary in context. You read a short text with 8 gaps and choose A, B, C or D for each. The four words usually have similar meanings — you have to pick the one that fits the exact context, collocation or fixed phrase.

Length
~150 words
Questions
8 (each worth 1 mark)
Time
~8 minutes
Skills tested
Vocabulary, collocations, phrasal verbs, idioms

Strategy

  • Read the whole text first — fast — to get the topic
  • Read the FULL sentence around each gap, not just the words next to it
  • Look for collocations: which word combines naturally with the others?
  • Check if it is a phrasal verb (out, up, off, on, in, away, over)
  • Check if it is a fixed expression (take part, make sense, pay attention)
  • If unsure, eliminate options that clearly do not work, then guess

Common traps

Synonyms
Words like say/tell/speak/talk all have similar meanings but different uses.
Collocations
Make a decision (not do a decision). You have to know the right partner word.
Prepositions
Depend ON, interested IN, afraid OF — the choice often depends on the preposition that follows.
