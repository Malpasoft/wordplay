Writing Section Overview

Part 1 essay · Part 2 choice (article, email, letter, report, review)

Format and timing

Part 1
Compulsory essay · 140–190 words · ~40 minutes
Part 2
Choose ONE from: article, email/letter, report, review · 140–190 words · ~40 minutes
Total
2 tasks in 80 minutes · 20% of total exam

Part 1 — The essay (compulsory)

You are given a topic, a question to discuss, two prompts (notes), and you must add a third idea of your own.

  • Always answer the question fully — do not drift off topic
  • Use both given prompts and add your own third idea
  • Use formal register — no contractions, no slang
  • Use linking words: however, furthermore, moreover, on the other hand
  • Plan before writing: introduction, two arguments, conclusion

Part 2 — Choice tasks

Article
Engaging tone · question hook · personal voice
Email/Letter
Match the register to the recipient (formal vs informal)
Report
Headed sections · impersonal tone · recommendations
Review
Brief description + opinion + recommendation
