Part 2 — Open Cloze

8 questions · grammar focus · one word per gap

What does Part 2 test?

Part 2 has 8 gaps in a short text. You write ONE word in each gap. The answer is usually a small grammatical word — articles, prepositions, auxiliaries, pronouns, conjunctions, comparatives.

Length
~150 words
Questions
8 (each worth 1 mark)
Time
~10 minutes
Skills tested
Grammar — articles, prepositions, modals, comparatives, relative pronouns

Most common answer types

Articles
a, an, the
Prepositions
on, in, at, of, by, for, with, from, into
Auxiliary verbs
has, have, had, was, were, do, does, did, would, will
Relative pronouns
who, which, that, whose, where, when
Pronouns
it, this, that, these, those
Conjunctions
although, despite, however, because, since, if, unless
Comparative words
as, than, more, less
Quantifiers
much, many, few, little, some, any, no

Strategy

  • Read the whole text first to understand it
  • For each gap, ask: what type of word fits? (preposition? article? auxiliary?)
  • Use the surrounding words as clues
  • Try your answer in the sentence — does it sound natural?
  • Spelling matters! Recieve is wrong; receive is right
  • Only ONE word — never write two words
