Can — la capacité et l'autorisation

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la forme correcte avec can
6 points
Attention à l'infinitif sans to et à l'absence de -s.
1
My brother ______ play the guitar very well.
2
______ you speak Spanish?
3
I ______ find my keys — have you seen them?
4
She ______ very well in the dark.
5
______ I borrow your pen, please?
6
They ______ here — it's a no-parking zone.
Exercice 2
Complète avec can ou can't
5 points
Écris can ou can't selon le sens.
1
I ______ ride a bike — I never learned. (ne sais pas)
2
______ your sister cook? Yes, she ______ make amazing pasta. (can / can)
3
Sorry, I ______ come tonight — I have too much homework. (ne peut pas)
4
______ you open the window, please? It's so hot! (peut-tu)
5
He ______ speak three languages — French, English and Spanish! (sait)
