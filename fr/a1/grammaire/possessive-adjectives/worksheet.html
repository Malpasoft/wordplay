Exercice 2
Complète avec le bon possessif
5 points
Écris my, your, his, her, its, our ou their.
1
Is this ______ pen, or is it mine? (yours / ton)
2
Paul and Marie love ______ dog. ______ name is Rex. (their / its)
3
I can't find ______ keys. Have you seen them? (my)
4
Sophie broke ______ arm last week. (her)
5
The school is proud of ______ students. (its)
