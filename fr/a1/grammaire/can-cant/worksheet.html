Can/can't — permissions et refus

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Can ou can't ?
6 points
Permission ou interdiction ? Complète avec can ou can't.
1
______ I use your phone for a second?
2
Sorry, you ______ eat in the library.
3
______ we leave the class five minutes early today?
4
You ______ park here — look at the sign!
5
Of course you ______ sit here — the seat is free!
6
Students ______ use their phones during the exam.
