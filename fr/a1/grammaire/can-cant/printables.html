Forme Exemple

Accorder permissionYou can + base verbale.You can use my pen.
Refuser / interdireYou can't + base verbale.You can't eat here.
Règle formellecannot + base verbaleYou cannot enter without a ticket.
Réponse positiveYes, you can. / Of course!Yes, you can go.
Réponse négativeNo, you can't. / Sorry, no.No, you can't park here.
