Le present continuous — I am working

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Forme le present continuous
6 points
be + verbe-ing : les deux parties comptent.
1
Look! It ______ ! (rain)
2
I ______ my homework right now. (do)
3
They ______ football in the park. (play)
4
She ______ a cake at the moment. (make)
5
The children ______ in the garden. (run)
6
He ______ to music. (listen)
Exercice 2
Simple ou continuous ?
5 points
Choisis le bon temps et écris le verbe.
1
I usually ______ tea, but today I ______ coffee. (drink / drink)
2
She ______ in a hospital. C'est son métier. (work)
3
Be quiet! The baby ______. (sleep)
4
I ______ this song! (love)
5
What ______ you ______ right now? (do)
Exercice 3
Corrige les fautes
5 points
Choisis la version correcte.
1

2

3
« I am wanting a pizza. »
4
« Do they playing tennis? »
5
« He is studying every day. » (habitude !)
