Have got — j'ai, tu as, il a...

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la bonne forme
6 points
Have got ou has got ? Fais attention à la personne.
1
My sister ______ a new phone.
2
______ you got a pen I can borrow?
3
We ______ a lot of homework tonight.
4
He ______ any brothers or sisters.
5
How old ______ you?
6
I ______ two tickets for the concert!
Exercice 2
Complète les phrases
5 points
Écris la forme correcte de have got ou to be pour l'âge.
1
My dog ______ big brown eyes. (has got)
2
______ your brother got a car? Yes, he ______ . (have / has)
3
How old ______ your teacher? She ______ about 35. (is / is)
4
We ______ any milk. Can you buy some? (haven't got)
5
My parents ______ a beautiful garden. (have got)
