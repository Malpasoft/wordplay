Leçon Exercices Jeu

Les pronoms sujets — I, you, he, she, it, we, they

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon pronom sujet
6 points
Quel pronom remplace le groupe nominal ?
1
Marie is a student. ______ is 18.
2
The book is great. ______ is very funny.
3
Tom and Alice are here. ______ are my friends.
4
My sister and ______ are from Lyon.
5
______ are students in the same class.
6
The chairs? ______ are in the living room.
Exercice 2

2

3
Where is my phone? ______ is on the table.
4

5
