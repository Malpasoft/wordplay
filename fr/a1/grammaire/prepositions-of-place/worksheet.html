Les prépositions de lieu — in, on, under, next to

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la bonne préposition
6 points
In, on, under, next to, behind, in front of ou between ?
1
The book is ______ the table. (sur)
2
The keys are ______ the bag. (dans)
3
The cat is ______ the bed. (sous)
4
The bank is ______ the shop and the café. (entre)
5
The garden is ______ the house. (derrière)
6
The bus stop is ______ the school. (devant)

4

5
