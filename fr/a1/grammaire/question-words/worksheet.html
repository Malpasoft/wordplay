Leçon Exercices Jeu Imprimables

Les mots interrogatifs — who, what, where, when, why, how

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon mot interrogatif
6 points
Qui, quoi, où, quand, pourquoi ou comment ?
1
______ do you live? — In Lyon.
2
______ is your birthday? — In May.
3
______ is that woman? — She's my teacher.
4
______ are you learning English? — Because I love it!
5
______ do you go to school? — By bus.
6
______ is your favourite colour? — Blue.
Exercice 2
Complète la question
5 points
Écris le mot qui manque (auxiliaire ou mot interrogatif).
1
Where ______ you live? (auxiliaire)
2
How old ______ you? (verbe)
3
What ______ she do at the weekend? (auxiliaire)
4
How ______ brothers do you have? (combien, dénombrable)
5
______ bag is this? It's Marie's. (à qui)
Exercice 3
Corrige les questions fausses
5 points
Choisis la version correcte.
1
« Where you go on holiday? »
2
« How old have you? »
3
« What time it is? »
4
« How much books do you have? »
5
