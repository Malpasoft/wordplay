Exercice 2
Complète avec am, is, are ou la contraction
5 points
Écris la forme correcte du verbe to be.
1
My name ______ Sophie and I ______ fifteen years old. (is / am)
2
______ your parents at home? No, they ______ not. (are / are)
3
This ______ my friend Lucas. He ______ a great footballer. (is / is)
4
We ______ in the same class. ______ you in class 3B? (are / are)
5
It ______ cold today! The windows ______ very cold too. (is / are)
