Exercice 2
Transforme en question indirecte
5 points
Écris le ou les mots qui manquent.
1
Where is the station? → Can you tell me where the station ______?
2
Where does she work? → Do you know where she ______? (le -s revient !)
3
Is this seat free? → Do you know ______ this seat is free? (si)
4
When did the train leave? → Can you tell me when the train ______? (au passé)
5
What time do the shops close? → I'd like to know what time the shops ______.
