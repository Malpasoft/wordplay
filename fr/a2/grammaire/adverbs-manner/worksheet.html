Leçon Exercices Jeu Imprimables

Les adverbes de manière — slowly, well, hard

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Adjectif ou adverbe ?
6 points
Décide si le mot décrit un nom ou un verbe.
1
She is a ______ driver.
2
She drives ______.
3
He speaks English very ______.
4
My grandfather walks very ______.
5
She passed the exam ______.
6
Be ______! The floor is wet.
Exercice 2
Forme l'adverbe
5 points
Écris l'adverbe correspondant à l'adjectif.
1
quiet → Please close the door ______.
2
good → She plays the piano very ______. (irrégulier !)
3
happy → The children played ______ in the garden.
4
fast → He runs very ______. (attention, pas de -ly !)
5
hard → They worked ______ all week. (attention, pas de -ly !)
