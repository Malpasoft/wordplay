Les quantifieurs — a lot of, a few, a little

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon quantifieur
6 points
Dénombrable ou indénombrable ? Affirmatif ou négatif ?
1
She has ______ friends at school.
2
I don't have ______ money this month.
3
There are only ______ eggs left.
4
Can I have ______ milk in my coffee?
5
How ______ apples do we need for the cake?
6
There aren't ______ hotels in this village.
