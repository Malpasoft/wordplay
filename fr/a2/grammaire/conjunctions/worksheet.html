Leçon Exercices Jeu Imprimables

Les conjonctions — and, but, so, because

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la bonne conjonction
6 points
Cause, conséquence, contraste ou addition ?
1
I was tired, ______ I went to bed early.
2
I went to bed early ______ I was tired.
3
She likes music ______ she can't sing.
4
Do you want pasta ______ rice?
5
I bought bread, milk ______ eggs.
6
______ it rains tomorrow, we'll stay at home.
Exercice 2
Complète la phrase
5 points
Écris la conjonction ou la forme verbale qui manque.
1
It was cold, ______ I took my coat. (donc)
2
I love this song ______ the lyrics are beautiful. (parce que)
3
I'll call you when I ______ home. (arriver — attention au temps !)
4
If it ______ sunny, we'll go to the beach. (être — attention au temps !)
5
______ we visited the museum. Then we had lunch. (d'abord)
Exercice 3
Corrige les fautes de liaison
5 points
Choisis la version correcte.
1
« If it will rain, we will cancel the picnic. »
2
« I'll text you when I will arrive at the station. »
3
« He was hungry, because he made a sandwich. »
4
« I like swimming and I hate running. »
5
« Finally, we visited the castle. Then we arrived. First we left. » (ordre !)
