Narrative Tenses — Raconter comme un pro

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon temps du récit
6 points
Action, décor ou avant-histoire ?
1
When I opened the curtains, the sun ______ .
2
We arrived late because we ______ the wrong train.
3
He was out of breath — he ______ for an hour.
4
As soon as she saw the spider, she ______ .
5
While we ______ , the children were playing on the beach.
6
______ , after three hours of waiting, the plane took off.
Exercice 2
Complète le récit
5 points
Écris la forme correcte du verbe.
1
It ______ hard when we left the house. (rain, décor)
2
By the time we got there, the meeting ______ . (end, avant nous)
3
She was angry because she ______ waiting for an hour. (be — durée antérieure)
4
Suddenly, the lights ______ out. (go, action)
5
______ , we found the keys under the sofa. (finalement)

2

3

4
« Je viendrai éventuellement. » (peut-être)
5
