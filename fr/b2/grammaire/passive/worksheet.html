Exercice 2
Complète la structure
5 points
Écris le mot manquant.
1
We had our kitchen ______ last month. (rénover — participe passé)
2
He is said ______ be the best player of his generation. (la particule)
3
My wallet ______ stolen on the metro. (se faire voler, à l’oral — prétérit)
4
I ______ told that the meeting was cancelled. (be au prétérit)
5
The documents need to ______ signed before Friday. (be)
