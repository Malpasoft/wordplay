Verb Patterns — Verbe + objet + infinitif

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la forme correcte
6 points
Verbe + objet + infinitif (avec ou sans to).
1
My boss wants ______ the report today.
2
The teacher made us ______ the exercise again.
3
Her parents won’t let her ______ to the party.
4
The school allows students ______ their phones at lunch.
5
I heard someone ______ my name.
6
He was made ______ for an hour.
Exercice 2
Complète le motif
5 points
Écris le mot manquant.
1
I’d like you ______ meet my parents. (la particule)
2
She taught her brother ______ swim. (la particule)
3
They didn’t ______ us take photos inside. (laisser)
4
The film ______ me cry. (faire — prétérit)
5
I expect everyone ______ be on time. (la particule)
