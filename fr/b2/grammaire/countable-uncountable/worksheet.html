Countable & Uncountable — Les indénombrables pièges

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la forme correcte
6 points
Dénombrable ou non ?
1
He gave me ______ about the visa.
2
The news ______ better than expected.
3
How ______ luggage are you taking?
4
Let me give you ______ of advice.
5
There were very ______ people at the meeting.
6
We still have ______ time — no need to rush.
Exercice 2
Complète la phrase
5 points
Écris le mot manquant.
1
Can you give me some ______ ? I don’t know what to choose. (des conseils)
2
Her ______ is dark and curly. (les cheveux)
3
We need to buy some new ______ for the flat. (des meubles)
4
They went on a ______ to Morocco last spring. (un voyage)
5
How ______ pieces of luggage can I check in? (combien)
