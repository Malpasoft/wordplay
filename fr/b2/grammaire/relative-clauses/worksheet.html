Relative Clauses — Relatives avancées

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la forme correcte
6 points
Relatives avancées.
1
My sister, ______ works in Paris, is visiting us.
2
That’s the woman ______ son won the competition.
3
The hotel ______ we stayed was right on the beach.
4
She passed her exam, ______ made her parents proud.
5
The colleagues ______ I have lunch are very friendly.
6
Anyone ______ a ticket can enter the draw.
Exercice 2
Complète la relative
5 points
Écris le mot manquant.
1
The keys ______ on the table are mine. (relative réduite passive de leave)
2
He’s the artist ______ paintings sell for millions. (dont)
3
It rained all week, ______ ruined our holiday. (ce qui)
4
The town ______ I grew up has changed a lot. (où)
5
To ______ should I address the letter? (à qui — soutenu)
