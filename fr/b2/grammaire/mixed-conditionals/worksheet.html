Mixed Conditionals — Mélanger les temps

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la bonne moitié
6 points
Passé ou présent, de chaque côté ?
1
If I had gone to bed earlier, I ______ so tired now.
2
If she ______ shy, she would have introduced herself at the meeting.
3
If we ______ the map, we wouldn’t be lost now.
4
If he spoke better English, he ______ that job last month.
5
If you hadn’t helped me, I ______ still ______ on it today.
6
If I ______ rich, I would have bought that villa years ago.
Exercice 2
Complète le conditionnel mixte
5 points
Écris la forme correcte.
1
If I had accepted the offer, I ______ be in London now. (would)
2
If you ______ listened to me, you wouldn’t be in trouble now. (had, à la forme négative complète : hadn’t... non — ici affirmative : had)
3
If she weren’t so stubborn, she would ______ apologised by now. (l’auxiliaire)
4
If we hadn’t sold the house, we ______ still live there. (would)
5
If I ______ afraid of flying, I would have visited you in Sydney. (be au prétérit négatif : weren’t)
