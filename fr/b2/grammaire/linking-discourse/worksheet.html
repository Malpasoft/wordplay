Linking & Discourse — Articuler son discours

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon connecteur
6 points
Nom ou proposition ? Une phrase ou deux ?
1
______ the heavy traffic, we arrived on time.
2
______ he trained hard, he lost the final.
3
The hotel was expensive. ______ , the service was poor.
4
The match was cancelled ______ the storm.
5
I see your point. ______ , I still disagree.
6
______ being injured, she finished the race.
Exercice 2
Complète le connecteur
5 points
Écris le mot manquant.
1
In spite ______ the noise, the baby slept. (le petit mot)
2
Despite the fact ______ it was late, they kept playing. (avec une proposition)
3
He failed the exam. ______ , he can retake it in June. (heureusement : cependant)
4
______ you’re asking, I’ll tell you the truth. (puisque)
5
To sum ______ , the project deserves our support. (pour conclure)
