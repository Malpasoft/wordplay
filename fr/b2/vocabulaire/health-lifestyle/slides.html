B2 Vocabulary

Health and Lifestyle Vocabulary

well-being
general health, happiness and comfort
nutrition
the process of getting the right food for good health
sedentary lifestyle
a way of life involving little physical activity
obesity
the condition of being seriously overweight
chronic illness
a long-lasting health condition
mental health
a person's psychological and emotional state

More health vocabulary

preventable disease
an illness that could be avoided with the right lifestyle
healthcare
the organised provision of medical services
fitness regime
a regular programme of exercise
work-life balance
having enough time for both work and personal life
immune system
the body's defence against illness
burnout
exhaustion caused by prolonged stress and overwork

Key collocations

improve / promote well-being
maintain a fitness regime
achieve a work-life balance
tackle obesity / mental health issues
boost the immune system
access healthcare services
