Health and Lifestyle

Wellness, nutrition, fitness, mental health

Upper-intermediate vocabulary · Health expressions · Lifestyle terms
B2 Vocabulary

Physical health

metabolism
the chemical process that converts food to energy
immunity
the body's ability to resist disease
nutrition
the study of food and its effects on the body
cardiovascular
relating to the heart and blood vessels
vaccination
inoculation with a vaccine to prevent disease
wellness
the state of being in good physical health

Mental and lifestyle

mindfulness
being aware of the present moment
meditation
focused mental practice for relaxation
anxiety
feeling of worry or unease
stress management
techniques to cope with pressure
sleep hygiene
habits that promote good sleep quality
lifestyle
the way someone lives their life

Habits and wellness

sustainable
able to be maintained over time
holistic
relating to the whole person or system
preventative
designed to stop disease before it occurs
supplement
an additional product to enhance diet
regime
a structured system or routine
discipline
self-control and adherence to a regimen

B2-specific language

Collocations
maintain health, adopt a lifestyle, combat disease, boost immunity
Phrases
take preventative measures, lead a healthy life, manage stress effectively
Adjectives
holistic, sustainable, mindful, nutritious, preventative
