Passive — Le passif au présent et au prétérit

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la forme passive correcte
6 points
Attention à l’accord de be et au participe.
1
These watches ______ in Switzerland.
2
The castle ______ in the 12th century.
3
The Mona Lisa ______ by Leonardo da Vinci.
4
Portuguese ______ in Brazil.
5
My keys ______ somewhere in the park.
6
When ______ this photo ______ ?
Exercice 2
Mets au passif
5 points
Écris la forme passive demandée.
1
They make this cheese in Normandy. → This cheese ______ ______ in Normandy.
2
Somebody stole my bike. → My bike ______ ______ .
3
They built these houses in 1950. → These houses ______ built in 1950. (be)
4
Shakespeare wrote Hamlet. → Hamlet was written ______ Shakespeare. (par)
5
On m’a invité à la fête. → I ______ invited to the party.
