Present Perfect — Ce n’est pas le passé composé

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Present perfect ou past simple ?
6 points
Choisis la forme correcte — attention aux indications de temps.
1
I ______ this film three times.
2
She ______ to Italy last summer.
3
We ______ here since 2019.
4
______ you ever ______ sushi?
5
He ______ his keys — he can’t open the door.
6
They ______ the project in 2020.
Exercice 2
Complète au present perfect
5 points
Écris la forme correcte du verbe entre parenthèses.
1
I ______ my homework — can I watch TV? (finish)
2
She ______ in Lyon for five years. (live)
3
They ______ already ______ . (leave)
4
______ you ______ the new café yet? (try)
5
I ______ never ______ to Ireland. (be)
