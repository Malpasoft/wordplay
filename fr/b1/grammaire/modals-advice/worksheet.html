Modals of Advice — Should, ought to, had better

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon conseil
6 points
Nuance neutre, formelle ou urgente ?
1
You ______ eat more vegetables.
2
It’s icy — you ______ drive slowly!
3
You ______ to thank them for the invitation.
4
______ I tell her the truth?
5
You’d better ______ forget your passport.
6
Maybe you ______ talk to your boss about it.
Exercice 2
Complète la phrase
5 points
Écris le mot qui manque.
1
You ______ better hurry — we’re late! (ferais mieux de)
2
What ______ I wear to the wedding? (devrais)
3
You ought ______ apologise to her. (le petit mot manquant)
4
Why ______ you take a day off? (suggestion : et si... ?)
5
You should ______ go to bed so late. (négation du conseil)
