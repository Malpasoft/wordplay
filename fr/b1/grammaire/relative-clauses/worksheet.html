Relative Clauses — Who, which, that

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis le bon relatif
6 points
Personne, chose, possession, lieu ou temps ?
1
The doctor ______ treated me was very kind.
2
The keys ______ were on the table have disappeared.
3
That’s the singer ______ album sold millions.
4
The village ______ my grandparents live is beautiful.
5
I’ll never forget the summer ______ we met.
6
The book ______ you lent me is fascinating.
Exercice 2
Complète avec le relatif
5 points
Écris le mot qui manque (ou rien n’est pas proposé ici).
1
The man ______ fixed our roof is retired. (qui)
2
The hotel ______ we stayed was right on the beach. (où)
3
She’s the friend ______ advice I always trust. (dont les conseils)
4
The day ______ I passed my exam was unforgettable. (où, temporel)
5
Everything ______ he said was true. (ce que... → tout ce qu’il a dit)
