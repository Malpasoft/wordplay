Past Perfect — Le plus-que-parfait

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Choisis la forme correcte
6 points
Quelle action est la plus ancienne ?
1
When I got home, my family ______ dinner.
2
She ______ never ______ sushi before she went to Japan.
3
After he ______ his homework, he watched TV.
4
The meeting ______ by the time we arrived.
5
I didn’t laugh because I ______ the joke before.
6
They ______ the house before the price went up.
Exercice 2
Complète au past perfect
5 points
Écris la forme correcte du verbe.
1
The film ______ already ______ when we sat down. (start)
2
She told me she ______ my email. (not / receive → forme négative complète « had not received » ou contractée)
3
By the time the police arrived, the thief ______ . (escape → échapper : escaped)
4
I was tired because I ______ badly. (sleep)
5
______ you ever ______ abroad before that trip? (live)
