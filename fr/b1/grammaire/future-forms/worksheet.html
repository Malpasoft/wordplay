Future Forms — Will, going to ou présent continu ?

Complète les exercices puis vérifie tes réponses. Total : 16 points.

Exercice 1
Will, going to ou présent continu ?
6 points
Choisis la forme la plus naturelle.
1
The phone’s ringing! — I ______ it.
2
We ______ married in June — everything is booked.
3
Watch out! You ______ that glass.
4
I promise I ______ you every day.
5
She ______ start a new job next month — she signed yesterday.
6
I’ll email you as soon as I ______ the results.
Exercice 2
Complète la phrase
5 points
Écris la forme demandée entre parenthèses.
1
Don’t worry, I ______ you with the boxes. (will + help)
2
Look at the sky — it ______ rain. (going to)
3
We ______ to Dublin on Friday — the tickets are booked. (fly, présent continu)
4
I’ll call you when I ______ home. (get)
5
They ______ probably ______ the match. (will + win)
Exercice 3

5
