3

4

5

6

Exercice 2
Complète le récit
5 points
Écris le mot manquant.
1
It was midnight ______ the phone rang. (quand)
2
She opened the door and realised someone ______ been inside. (l’auxiliaire de l’antériorité)
3
______ , a dog started barking. (soudain)
4
At ______ , I thought it was a joke — but I was wrong. (au début)
5
He grabbed his coat and ______ outside. (se précipiter — prétérit)
