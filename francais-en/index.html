What makes this course different?

  • English explanations — all grammar rules explained from an English speaker's perspective
  • Faux amis — false friends that trap English speakers (e.g. actually ≠ actuellement)
  • Gender rules — the system English lacks, explained with mnemonics
  • No "do-support" — French doesn't use do/does/did in questions or negatives
  • Pronunciation focus — liaison, silent letters, nasal vowels explained
  • DELF preparation — exam-style practice from A1
