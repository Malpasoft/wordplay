Complete the exercises, then check your answers. Total: 16 points.

Exercise 1
Identifying Critical Analysis Moves
6 points
Choose the best critical analysis phrase to complete each sentence.
1

2
You want to point out a logical weakness in an argument. Which is the best choice?
3
Which sentence correctly identifies a rhetorical technique?
4
You have found that the author uses statistics selectively. How do you express this?
5

6
Which phrase best opens a conclusion that reflects beyond the text?

2
