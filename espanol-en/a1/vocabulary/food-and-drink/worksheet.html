Food & Drink — Worksheet

Flashcards MatchLessonReviewGame
1. Translate to Spanish: "bread"
Answer: el pan

Explanation: “el pan” means “bread”. Example: Compro pan cada día.

2. Translate to Spanish: "milk"
Answer: la leche

Explanation: “la leche” means “milk”. Example: Bebo leche con el desayuno.

3. What does “el agua” mean?
Answer: water

Explanation: “el agua” = “water”. Example: Un vaso de agua, por favor.

4. What does “el café” mean?
Answer: coffee

Explanation: “el café” = “coffee”. Example: Me gusta el café con leche.

5. Complete: “El _______ manchego es famoso.” (cheese)
Answer: queso

Explanation: The missing word is “queso” (cheese): El queso manchego es famoso.

6. Complete: “Como _______ todos los días.” (fruit)
Answer: fruta

Explanation: The missing word is “fruta” (fruit): Como fruta todos los días.
