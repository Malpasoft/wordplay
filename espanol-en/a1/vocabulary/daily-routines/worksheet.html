1. Translate to Spanish: "to wake up"
Answer: despertarse

Explanation: “despertarse” means “to wake up”. Example: Me despierto a las siete.

2. Translate to Spanish: "to get up"
Answer: levantarse

Explanation: “levantarse” means “to get up”. Example: Me levanto en seguida.

3. What does “ducharse” mean?
Answer: to shower

Explanation: “ducharse” = “to shower”. Example: Me ducho por la mañana.

4. What does “vestirse” mean?
Answer: to get dressed

Explanation: “vestirse” = “to get dressed”. Example: Me visto en cinco minutos.

5. Complete: “_______ café y tostadas.” (to have breakfast)
Answer: Desayuno

Explanation: The missing word is “Desayuno” (to have breakfast): Desayuno café y tostadas.

6. Complete: “_______ de nueve a cinco.” (to work)
Answer: Trabajo

Explanation: The missing word is “Trabajo” (to work): Trabajo de nueve a cinco.
