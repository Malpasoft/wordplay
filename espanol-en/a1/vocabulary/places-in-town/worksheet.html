Places in Town — Worksheet

FlashcardsLessonReviewGame
1. Translate to Spanish: "city"
Answer: la ciudad

Explanation: “la ciudad” means “city”. Example: Vivo en una ciudad grande.

2. Translate to Spanish: "bank"
Answer: el banco

Explanation: “el banco” means “bank”. Example: El banco abre a las nueve.

3. What does “el supermercado” mean?
Answer: supermarket

Explanation: “el supermercado” = “supermarket”. Example: Compro en el supermercado.

4. What does “la farmacia” mean?
Answer: pharmacy

Explanation: “la farmacia” = “pharmacy”. Example: La farmacia está cerca.

5. Complete: “El _______ es nuevo.” (hospital)
Answer: hospital

Explanation: The missing word is “hospital” (hospital): El hospital es nuevo.

6. Complete: “Los niños van a la _______.” (school)
Answer: escuela

Explanation: The missing word is “escuela” (school): Los niños van a la escuela.
