Part 4 — Key Word Transformation

Six questions. Use BETWEEN 3 AND 8 words INCLUDING the key word (which cannot be changed). Tests advanced grammar transformations.

Strategy

  • The two sentences must mean EXACTLY the same
  • Common at CPE: inverted conditionals (Had I known...), cleft sentences, advanced reporting verbs
  • Watch idiomatic structures: "no sooner... than", "as if...", "wish + past perfect"
  • 2 points per question — extra valuable
  • Count words carefully; contractions = 2 words
  • Time guide: 20 minutes
