Part 6 — Gapped Text

A long text with seven paragraphs removed. Place them back in order. One paragraph is a distractor.

Strategy

  • Read the gapped text first to follow the argument's flow
  • Each missing paragraph must link to BOTH the preceding and following text
  • Watch for cohesion clues: pronouns, linking words, lexical chains, topic continuity
  • One paragraph won't fit anywhere — eliminate it once you're sure
  • Re-read the whole text after answering to verify the flow
  • Time guide: 18 minutes
