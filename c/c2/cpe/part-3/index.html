Part 3 — Word Formation

Eight gaps, with a root word in capitals to transform. CPE requires complex changes: multiple affixes, negative prefixes, sometimes irregular forms.

Strategy

  • Identify the required word class precisely
  • Check for both prefix AND suffix changes (e.g. ABLE → INABILITY)
  • Negative prefixes are very common: un-, in-, dis-, mis-, ir-, im-, il-, non-
  • Look out for plurals and tense forms
  • Some answers are compound: NIGHT → OVERNIGHT, FALL → DOWNFALL
  • Time guide: 12 minutes
