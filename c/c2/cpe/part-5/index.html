6 Qs · long text

Part 5 — Reading Multiple Choice

One long text (about 800 words), six 4-option questions. Tests detailed comprehension, inference, attitude, complex vocabulary in context.

Strategy

  • Read once for overall meaning, then again per question
  • Questions follow text order — find the relevant paragraph for each
  • The wrong options often paraphrase the text but distort the meaning subtly
  • Look for the writer's attitude through word choice, tone, irony
  • Time guide: 15 minutes
