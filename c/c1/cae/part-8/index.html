Part 8 — Multiple Matching

One long text divided into sections (or several short texts) and 10 questions. Match each question to the section that mentions it.

Strategy

  • Read the questions FIRST — they tell you what to scan for
  • Underline keywords in the questions before scanning
  • Scan the text quickly looking for paraphrases of those keywords
  • Same section can be the answer to multiple questions
  • Don't get stuck — return to difficult questions at the end
  • Time guide: 15 minutes
