Part 4 — Key Word Transformation

Complete the second sentence using 3-6 words including the key word. Do NOT change the key word.

I regret not accepting the job offer.

I the job offer.

2

SAID

The castle in the 14th century.

3

She started learning French three years ago.

BEEN

She three years.

4

It was such a boring film that we left early.

SO

The film we left early.

5

"You should see a doctor," she told me.

ADVISED

6
