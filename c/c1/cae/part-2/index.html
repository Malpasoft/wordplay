Part 2 — Open Cloze

Eight gaps to fill with ONE word each. Tests grammar: articles, prepositions, auxiliaries, pronouns, linking words, and small grammatical items.

Strategy

  • Most answers are grammar words: articles (a/the/no), pronouns (it/them/which), prepositions, auxiliaries
  • Common answers: that, which, this, no, none, any, some, in, of, by, for, even, despite, having, been
  • If you can write only one word, check carefully — never write contractions like "don't"
  • Read each sentence aloud after filling the gap — does it sound natural?
  • Time guide: 12 minutes
