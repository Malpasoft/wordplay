Part 7 — Gapped Text

A long text with six paragraphs removed and one extra distractor. You must place the 6 paragraphs back into the correct gaps.

Strategy

  • Read the main text first to follow the argument before looking at the options
  • Each paragraph must fit logically with what comes before AND after
  • Look for cohesion clues: pronouns (he/she/it/they/this), linking words, and topic continuity
  • One paragraph is a distractor — it will fit nowhere
  • Re-read the whole text after answering to verify it flows
  • Time guide: 15 minutes
