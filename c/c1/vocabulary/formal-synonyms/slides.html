Formal Synonyms

commence · endeavour · facilitate · obtain · regarding · sufficient

Flashcards Lesson Game

Words 1–4

Commence
to begin (formal equivalent of "start")
Endeavour
to try hard to do or achieve something (formal equivalent of "try")
Facilitate
to make something easier or possible (formal equivalent of "help")
Obtain
to get or acquire something (formal equivalent of "get")
Examples
The meeting will commence at nine o'clock sharp.
We will endeavour to resolve the matter as soon as possible.

Words 5–8

Regarding
concerning; with respect to (formal equivalent of "about")
Sufficient
enough for the purpose (formal equivalent of "enough")
Subsequently
after something; afterwards (formal equivalent of "later")
Prior to
before (formal equivalent of "before")
Examples
I am writing regarding your recent application.
There is not sufficient evidence to support the claim.

Words 9–10

Pertaining to
relating to or connected with (formal equivalent of "relating to")
Acknowledge
to accept or admit that something is true (formal equivalent of "admit")
Examples
Please refer to the section pertaining to data protection.
The report acknowledges significant room for improvement.
