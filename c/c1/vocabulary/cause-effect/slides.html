Cause and Effect

consequently · therefore · stems from · brings about · leads to

Flashcards Lesson Game

Words 1–4

Consequently
as a result; therefore
Therefore
for that reason; as a logical conclusion
Stems from
originates from; has its source in
Brings about
causes something to happen; produces a change
Examples
Funding was cut. Consequently, several programmes were abandoned.
The evidence is inconclusive. Therefore, no firm conclusions can be drawn.

Words 5–8

Leads to
results in; causes something to happen
Contributes to
is one of the factors that causes or helps produce something
Results in
produces or causes a particular outcome
Gives rise to
causes or produces something, often something new or negative
Examples
Prolonged stress can lead to serious health complications.
Poor diet contributes to a higher risk of chronic disease.

Words 9–10

Underpins
supports or provides the basis for something
Triggers
causes something to start or happen, often suddenly
Examples
Trust underpins all successful professional relationships.
A minor incident can trigger widespread social unrest.
