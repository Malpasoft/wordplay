Exercise 2
Rewrite as a cleft sentence emphasising the underlined element
4 points
1
[The noise] woke me up. (use it-cleft)
2
[Hard work] leads to success. (use wh-cleft)
3
I just want [honesty]. (use all-cleft)
4
[The manager] made that decision. (use it-cleft)
