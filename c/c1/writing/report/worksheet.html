Exercise 1
Identify the report section
4 points
1
“The purpose of this report is to examine student satisfaction with the college sports centre and make recommendations for improvement.”
2
“A survey of 150 students was conducted in November. The data indicates that 72% of respondents were satisfied with the facilities, but only 40% found the opening hours convenient.”
3
“It is advisable that the centre extend its opening hours to include early mornings. Steps should also be taken to add two additional treadmills.”
4
“Taking into account all the findings, targeted investment would significantly enhance the student experience.”
Exercise 2
Choose the correct formal report language
6 points
1
The ______ of this report is to assess the effectiveness of the new training programme.
2
The data ______ that attendance has improved significantly since the restructuring.
3
It is ______ that a new pre-booking system be introduced for equipment.
4
______ into account all the evidence, the programme has been broadly successful.
5
Steps should ______ to address the issue of overcrowding at peak times.
6
It is ______ recommended that the committee consider these findings carefully.
