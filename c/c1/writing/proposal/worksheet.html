Exercise 1
Identify the proposal section
4 points
1
“The aim of this proposal is to recommend measures that would improve the cycling infrastructure in the city centre.”
2
“Currently, there are fewer than twelve kilometres of dedicated cycle lanes in the city. This has resulted in a significant number of cycling accidents each year.”
3
“It is recommended that the council invest in an additional forty kilometres of protected cycle lanes. Furthermore, a public bike-share scheme should be established.”
4
“In conclusion, targeted investment in cycling infrastructure would reduce congestion, improve public health, and lower carbon emissions. It is hoped that the committee will give these proposals serious consideration.”
