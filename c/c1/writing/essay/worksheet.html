Exercise 1
Identify the paragraph function
4 points
1
"Nowadays, many people argue that technology has fundamentally changed the way we learn. Should schools embrace digital tools, or do traditional methods remain superior?"
2
"Firstly, there is a strong case for integrating technology. Interactive tools can personalise learning and engage students who struggle with traditional methods."
3
"On the other hand, critics argue that excessive screen time is detrimental to concentration. Furthermore, face-to-face interaction with teachers remains irreplaceable."
4
"On balance, a blended approach appears most effective. Digital tools should complement, rather than replace, the classroom environment."
